SP INVESTMENT AND DEVELOPMENT COMPANY PVT LTD is a Private company incorporated December 1988.

As per the Companies Act, 1956, a Section 25 company — similar to what is defined under Section 8 under Companies Act, 2013 — is a not-for-profit charitable company formed with the sole object of “promoting commerce, art, science, religion, charity, or any other useful object, and intends to apply its profits.

At SPIDC, we believe in the power of collective action to create positive and lasting change in the world. Founded with a vision of a more equitable and compassionate society, we are a social welfare company dedicated to addressing pressing issues and improving the well-being of individuals and communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to foster social welfare by championing initiatives that empower, uplift, and transform lives. We are committed to creating sustainable solutions that tackle the root causes of societal challenges, working towards a future where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Focus on Impact-Driven Approach, Every project, program, and partnership is meticulously designed to maximize positive outcomes. We measure success not just in numbers but in the tangible improvements in the lives of those we serve.

We believe in the strength of partnerships. By collaborating with Govt organizations and other organization. businesses, and individuals, we amplify our impact and create a network of change-makers dedicated to social welfare.

Openness and transparency are at the core of our values. We keep our stakeholders informed about our activities, progress, and challenges, fostering trust and accountability.

Our work spans a diverse range of social issues, including but not limited to: Empowering individuals through education to break the cycle of poverty and build a more knowledgeable and skilled society.

Ensuring access to quality healthcare services and promoting wellness for all, irrespective of socio-economic status.

Investing in sustainable community projects that enhance infrastructure, economic opportunities, and overall well-being.

Recognizing the interdependence of social and environmental well-being, we actively pursue initiatives that promote ecological sustainability.
